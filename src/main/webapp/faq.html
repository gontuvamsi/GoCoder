GoCoder FAQ - Account and Login
1. How do I create an account on GoCoder?
To create an account, simply go to the GoCoder home page and click on the "Sign Up" or "Create Account" button. You will need to choose whether you are a Customer/Vendor or a Coder and provide required information like your name, email, and other details.
2. I am a Customer/Vendor. How do I login?
To login, click on the "Login" button located on the home page. Enter your registered email address and password, and click "Submit" to access your account.
3. I am a Coder. How do I login?
For Coders, the login process is the same as for Customers/Vendors. Click on the "Login" button on the home page, enter your credentials (email and password), and click "Submit."
4. What should I do if I forget my password?
If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password. You will receive an email with a password reset link.
5. How can I change my login credentials?
To change your login credentials (email or password), go to your account settings after logging in. There will be an option to update your email or password. Make sure to save your changes.
6. How do I log out of GoCoder?
To log out, simply click on the "Logout" button located in the navigation menu or your account settings. This will log you out of the platform and you will be returned to the home page.
7. Can I delete my account on GoCoder?
Currently, only the Administrator can delete accounts. If you wish to delete your account, you may contact the GoCoder support team, and they will assist you with the process.
8. What if I want to change my account type (from Coder to Customer/Vendor or vice versa)?
GoCoder does not currently offer the option to change account types. If you need to switch your role, you will have to create a new account with the desired role.
9. How do I reset my account details or update my profile?
  • Customers/Vendors can update their project details and other information when posting or editing projects.
  • Coders can update their profile by visiting the "Profile" section after logging in and making changes to their details.
10. Can I recover my account if it is blocked or deleted?
If your account is blocked or deleted by the Administrator, you will need to contact GoCoder support for assistance. They will provide information on the status of your account and how to proceed.
11. What happens if my account gets blocked?
  • Customers/Vendors: If your account is blocked, you will be temporarily unable to post projects or make changes to existing projects.
  • Coders: If your account is blocked, you will temporarily lose the ability to bid on new projects.
12. How can I contact support if I have issues with my login or account?
For any issues related to your login or account, you can contact GoCoder support through the "Contact Us" page or by emailing support@GoCoder.com. They will assist you with any problems you're facing.
13. How do I post a project or submit a bid after logging in?
  • Customers/Vendors: After logging in, you can click on "Post a Project" to submit your project details, such as project name, technology requirements, and target date.
  • Coders: Once logged in, you can search for projects, review available bids, and submit a quote for a project you're interested in.
14. Is my login information kept secure?
Yes, GoCoder employs secure methods to protect your login information and personal data. Always ensure that you are using a strong password and do not share your login credentials with others.
15. Can I have multiple accounts on GoCoder?
GoCoder allows users to have only one account per role (Customer/Vendor or Coder). Having multiple accounts under the same role is not recommended and could lead to account suspension.
16. How do I check my login history or account activity?
Currently, GoCoder does not provide a direct feature for viewing login history. However, you can check your account activity, such as project postings, quotes, and bids, by visiting your profile or account section.
GoCoder FAQ - Technical Issues
1. The page isnâ€™t loading. What should I do?
If the GoCoder page isn't loading, try the following steps:
  • Check your internet connection to ensure it's stable.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies. Sometimes outdated files can cause issues with loading pages.
  • Try a different browser or device to see if the issue persists.
  • Disable browser extensions that might be interfering with the page.
  • Refresh the page by pressing Ctrl + R (Windows) or Command + R (Mac).
  • Check GoCoder's status on social media or contact support to confirm if there's an ongoing outage.
If the issue persists, contact GoCoder support with details about the problem, including the browser and device you're using.
2. How do I report a bug?
To report a bug or technical issue on GoCoder, follow these steps:
  • Visit the "Contact Us" page on GoCoder and look for the "Report a Bug" section.
  • Provide a detailed description of the issue, including what you were trying to do when the bug occurred, any error messages, and steps to reproduce the issue if possible.
  • Attach screenshots or error logs if they are available, as this can help the technical team understand the problem.
  • Submit the form, and the GoCoder support team will get in touch with you to resolve the issue as soon as possible.
Alternatively, you can directly email support@GoCoder.com with a description of the bug you're experiencing.
GoCoder FAQ - Projects & Bids
1. How do I post a project?
To post a project as a Customer/Vendor on GoCoder, follow these steps:
  1. Login to your account using your registered email and password.
  2. Once logged in, navigate to your dashboard and select "Post a Project".
  3. Fill out the project details, including:
    • Project Name: Give your project a title.
    • Description: Provide a detailed description of the work required.
    • Application Development Environment: Specify the technologies or frameworks you'd like the coder to use.
    • Target Date: Mention the expected completion date.
  4. After entering all required information, click "Submit" to post your project.
Your project will then be available for coders to view and bid on.
2. How do I withdraw a bid?
If you're a Coder and wish to withdraw a bid you have submitted, follow these steps:
  1. Login to your account.
  2. Go to the "My Bids" section or your Dashboard where you can view your active bids.
  3. Find the project bid you wish to withdraw.
  4. Click on the "Withdraw" or "Cancel Bid" option next to the project.
  5. Confirm the withdrawal when prompted.
Once withdrawn, your bid will no longer be considered for that project, and you will not be able to submit further bids unless the project is re-opened for new quotes.
GoCoder FAQ - Payments
1. When will I receive my payment?
As a Coder, you will receive your payment once the customer has received the completed project and confirmed their satisfaction. The process is as follows:
  • Project completion: After you deliver the project to the customer, they will review it.
  • Customer approval: Once the customer is satisfied with the work, they will approve the project.
  • Payment release: After approval, the payment will be processed and transferred to your registered payment method.
Please note that the payment may be delayed if there are any issues with the project delivery or approval process. If you have any questions about payment, you can contact GoCoder support.
2. How do I update my payment method?
To update your payment method, follow these steps:
  1. Login to your account.
  2. Navigate to your Account Settings or Payment Settings section.
  3. Look for the option to Update Payment Method.
  4. Enter your new payment details (e.g., bank account, PayPal, etc.).
  5. Save the changes.
Ensure that your payment method is correctly updated to avoid any delays when receiving payments. If you encounter any issues, feel free to reach out to GoCoder support.
